Install on your phone

Home Screen install instructions

Open this section for the right steps for your device.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Open this site in Safari.
  2. Tap Share.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add.
  5. Open the Home Screen icon once while online, then test offline.

Android (Chrome / Edge)

  1. Open this site in Chrome or Edge.
  2. Open the browser menu.
  3. Choose Install app or Add to Home screen.
  4. Open the installed app once while online, then test offline.

Before the event

  • Open the app once with connectivity.
  • Create one test submission.
  • Turn on Airplane Mode.
  • Open the Home Screen app and save another test submission.
  • Export CSV to confirm your backup workflow works.
