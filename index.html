Home Screen install instructions

Open this section for the right steps for your device.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Open this site in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add.
  5. Open the new Home Screen icon once while online, then test offline.

Android (Chrome / Edge)

  1. Open this site in Chrome or Edge.
  2. Tap the browser menu, then choose Install app or Add to Home screen.
  3. Confirm the install.
  4. Open the app from your launcher once while online, then test offline.

Before the event

  • Open the app once with connectivity.
  • Create one test submission.
  • Turn on Airplane Mode.
  • Open the Home Screen app and save another test submission.
  • Export CSV to confirm your backup workflow works.
