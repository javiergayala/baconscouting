Pit Scouting Input Form Orlando — Offline App

This app mirrors the Google Form and saves submissions locally when you do not have connectivity. When you are back online, you can export the saved submissions as CSV or JSON and share them by email or import them into Google Sheets.

Source form: Google Form.

This app includes every visible field from the linked Google Form: Scout Name, Team, Drivetrain Type, Intake, Do they have auto?, Bumpers?, Climb in auto?, Need chute?, Teleop climb, Weight, How many fuel can they hold?, Feed from the neutral zone?, Can they play defense?, Fit under trench, and Notes.

Install on Your Phone

For the best offline experience, open this app in your phone browser while you still have connectivity, then add it to your Home Screen. After that, launch it from the Home Screen icon just like a normal app.

Checking your device for the best install instructions…
iPhone / iPad (Safari)
  1. Open this page in Safari.
  2. Let the page fully load once while online.
  3. Tap the Share button.
  4. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  5. Tap Add in the top-right corner.
  6. Open the app from your Home Screen for offline use.
Tip: If you do not see Add to Home Screen, make sure you opened the site directly in Safari rather than inside another app’s built-in browser.
Android (Chrome or Edge)
  1. Open this page in Chrome or Edge.
  2. Let the page fully load once while online.
  3. Look for an Install app, Add to Home screen, or Install prompt.
  4. If you do not see a prompt, tap the browser menu (usually the three dots).
  5. Choose Install app or Add to Home screen.
  6. Confirm the install, then launch it from your Home Screen or app drawer.
Before you rely on it offline
  1. Open the installed app once while you still have connectivity.
  2. Save a test submission.
  3. Turn on Airplane Mode and reopen the app from the Home Screen.
  4. Make sure the form still opens and you can save another test submission.
  5. Export a CSV so you know the whole workflow works on your device.
